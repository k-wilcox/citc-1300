Millie

History

Millie, the orange tabby queen of snacks and naps, is living her best life! She can't resist a good treat and spends most of her time snoozing away. When she's feeling playful, she's loves to play fetch with hair ties and zooming around. With her chill vibes and playful spirit, Millie is the ultimate mood lifter for her lucky owner.

Millie's Favorite Things

  • Sleeping for the majority of the day
  • Treats (Greenies Specifically)
  • Playing fetch with hair ties
